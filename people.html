Dr. Ammar Shaikhouni, MD, PhD

Principal Investigator

Dr. Shaikhouni is a pediatric neurosurgeon and neuroscientist at Nationwide Children's Hospital. His research focuses on the neural circuit mechanisms of pediatric epilepsy, with a particular emphasis on Dravet Syndrome and other genetic epilepsies. He completed his MD/PhD training with expertise in systems neuroscience and neural signal processing, and brings both clinical and translational research perspectives to the lab.

He is committed to mentoring the next generation of clinician-scientists and computational neuroscientists interested in pediatric neurological disorders.
